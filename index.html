    • Pong is one of the earliest and most iconic video games in history. It was created by Allan Alcorn and released by Atari in 1972.

    Pong is a two-dimensional sports game that simulates table tennis (ping-pong). Here's how it works:

    The game screen shows two paddles (one on each side of the screen) and a ball that moves back and forth. Each player controls a paddle, moving it vertically to hit the ball. The goal is to hit the ball past the opponent's paddle to score a point. The ball bounces off the top and bottom walls and the paddles. The game continues until a player reaches a certain number of points or indefinitely, depending on the version.
